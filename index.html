Who we are.

FRC team 9206 GRP Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) Team out of Grand Rapids, MI. The team was established in June 2023 at Grand River Preparatory Highschool, whose mission is to promote FIRST and STEM education opportunities in Grand Rapids, MI. We are a highschool-based team and open to students who attend our school.
